About Us

NISWARTH CARE CLUB AND TRUST (Regd. No. 1510/22) shines as a beacon of compassion, commitment, and transformative action in the social welfare landscape of India. As a nationally celebrated non-profit organization, we are on a mission to create a future where opportunity, dignity, and hope are accessible to all.

Since our founding, we have relentlessly worked to bridge socio-economic divides, uplift marginalized communities, and champion the cause of humanity. Through pioneering initiatives and deep-rooted community engagement, we empower the underserved, advocate for social justice, and foster enduring change.

With a formidable network of over 10,000+ dedicated volunteers and supporters, we have orchestrated life-changing initiatives such as:

  • Massive Food Distribution Drives: Delivering nourishment and combating hunger across vulnerable populations.
  • Healthcare Facilitation Programs: Ensuring critical healthcare access and spreading health literacy in remote areas.
  • Essential Kit Initiatives: Providing vital resources and emergency aid to families in times of crisis.

Our passionate team orchestrates a wide spectrum of operations including:

  • Strategic Community Outreach and Empowerment Campaigns
  • Fundraising and Resource Mobilization to Amplify Impact
  • Volunteer Recruitment, Training, and Leadership Development
  • Program Innovation in Education, Healthcare, and Social Upliftment
